About Luxora

Light, shaped by wood and precision.

We manufacture custom wooden hanging lights with premium wood finish, modern LED technology and elegant craftsmanship.

Every Luxora fixture begins as timber, not a template. Our craftsmen shape, sand and seal each piece by hand before it's fitted with a tri-colour LED engine engineered for decades of quiet, even light. The outcome is a hanging light that reads as sculpture in daylight and glows like warmth itself after dark — sized, finished and built to the exact brief of your space.
